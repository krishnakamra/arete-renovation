Why Choose Arete for Carpentry?

Fine carpentry is the art form that separates a good renovation from a truly exceptional one. It is the detail work — the crown moulding that crowns a room, the built-in bookcase that transforms a wall into architecture, the wainscoting that adds gravitas to a hallway — that leaves the lasting impression every homeowner deserves.

Arete's carpentry team brings decades of combined experience in both traditional hand-tool techniques and modern precision work. We build from your measurements and vision, crafting custom pieces that fit your specific space perfectly and reflect your personal aesthetic with authenticity and artistry.

Our Craftsmanship Approach

Our Approach

Measure Twice, Cut Once

Every piece starts with precise measurement and a detailed drawing. We discuss profiles, materials, and finishes with you before any wood is touched — preventing errors and ensuring perfect results from first fit to final nail.

Hardwood and Paint-Grade Options

Whether you want the warmth of stained oak, the elegance of painted maple, or the richness of black walnut, we work in the wood species and finish that suits your vision and your budget.

Factory-Tight Joints

Our joiners are obsessed with tight, gap-free joints and perfect mitre angles. The hallmark of master carpentry is what you cannot see — the invisible precision inside every joint and corner.

Site-Built Custom Fit

Unlike modular systems from big-box stores, our carpentry is built on site to your exact dimensions. Floors that slope, walls that bow, and ceilings that vary are all accommodated without compromise.

Primed for Paint or Stain

All our carpentry work is delivered properly primed and ready for finish coats. We can provide full finishing as part of the carpentry scope — one team, one consistent result, no trade coordination.

Architectural Cohesion

We study your home's existing architectural character and match or complement it accurately. Period homes get period-appropriate details; contemporary spaces get clean, geometric precision.
