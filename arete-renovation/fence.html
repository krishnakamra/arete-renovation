Why Choose Arete for Installation?

A well-built fence is more than a property boundary — it is an architectural element that defines your property's character from the street. Whether you are seeking privacy from neighbours, a safe enclosure for children and pets, or a decorative feature that complements your landscaping, Arete Renovation builds fences that accomplish all three with style and permanence.

Our Fence Build Approach

Our Approach

Post Setting Excellence

Every fence we build starts with properly concrete-set posts at the correct depth for Ontario's frost line. A fence is only as straight and level as its posts — we invest the time to get this critical foundation exactly right.

Material Honest Advice

Cedar looks beautiful initially but requires maintenance every 2-3 years. Vinyl never needs painting or staining. Composite offers a middle ground. We give you genuinely honest, agenda-free guidance on each material.

Level, Plumb, and Square

We use laser levels throughout installation to ensure every panel is perfectly level, every post is plumb, and every corner is square. These are the details that separate premium fence installation from average work.

Custom Gate Solutions

Gates are the weakest point of any fence system. Our custom gate builds use heavy-duty hardware, anti-sag kits, and proper structural framing to ensure your gate swings perfectly and stays adjusted for years to come.

Bylaw Knowledge

We understand Toronto fence bylaw requirements regarding heights, setback distances, and shared-fence agreements with neighbours. We guide you through the neighbourly and legal aspects of any fence project.

Weatherproof Longevity

All wood fence components are treated to resist moisture, insects, and UV degradation. We use hot-dipped galvanized hardware throughout — because the fasteners holding your fence together must be as durable as the wood itself.
