Who We Are

Craftsmanship
Without Compromise

Arete Renovation was founded on a single, unwavering principle: every home deserves extraordinary care. The word "arete" — drawn from ancient Greek — means excellence or virtue. It is not just our name; it is our standard.

From intricate custom carpentry to sweeping whole-home renovations, our team of seasoned tradespeople brings decades of combined expertise to every project. We serve the Greater Toronto Area with pride, treating every client's home as if it were our own.
