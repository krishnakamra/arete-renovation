Why Choose Arete for Improvement?

Your home is the single greatest investment you will ever make. Arete Renovation's home improvement services are designed to protect, enhance, and maximise that investment through thoughtful design, superior materials, and flawless execution. We do not just renovate — we reimagine.

Every project begins with a thorough consultation where we listen to your goals, lifestyle, and aesthetic preferences. We then develop a detailed plan that accounts for budget, timeline, structural considerations, and design cohesion across your entire home from entry to backyard.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Timelines vary by scope. A single-room renovation typically takes 1-3 weeks, while whole-home transformations can span 2-4 months. We provide a detailed schedule with milestones during your free consultation.

Permits are required for structural changes, electrical upgrades, and plumbing modifications. Our team handles all permit applications and city inspections on your behalf — included in your project cost.

For most projects you can remain in your home. We work room-by-room to minimise disruption, and advise you if temporary relocation would meaningfully accelerate the project timeline.

We conduct thorough pre-renovation assessments to identify potential issues before they become surprises. If something unexpected arises during construction, we document it, present your options, and never proceed without your approval.
