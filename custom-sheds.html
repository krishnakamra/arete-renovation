Why Choose Arete for Sheds?

A custom-built shed surpasses any prefabricated kit in every measurable way: structural integrity, longevity, appearance, and the ability to tailor every dimension to your specific needs. At Arete Renovation, we build sheds using the same construction standards and materials as our home renovation work — no compromises.

Whether you need a simple garden tool shed, a proper workshop, a bike storage shelter, or a she-shed retreat space, we design and build a custom outbuilding that fits your lot perfectly, meets zoning requirements, and complements your home's architectural character with purpose and style.

Our Approach

Permit-Ready Drawings

For sheds over 10 m² in Toronto, we prepare site plans and structural drawings for permit submission. We handle the entire administrative process end-to-end so you can focus on enjoying the result.

True Construction Standards

Unlike kit sheds assembled from thin pre-cut panels, our sheds are stick-framed with 2x4 or 2x6 lumber, properly sheathed, and built to withstand decades of Ontario freeze-thaw cycles without warping or failing.

Matched to Your Home

We design the shed's siding profile, roofline pitch, and trim details to complement your home's exterior. The result looks purposeful and deliberate — not an afterthought placed in your backyard.

Foundation Options

Depending on your soil conditions and intended use, we install pressure-treated wood skids, a concrete pad, or helical piers — the right foundation for your specific application and local frost depth requirements.

Workshop and Studio Ready

Need electrical, insulation, and a finished interior for a true workshop or creative studio? We wire, insulate, and finish shed interiors to the same standard as our home renovation work — fully functional, year-round spaces.

Designed to Last Decades

We use premium exterior-grade materials, stainless or hot-dipped galvanized fasteners, and proper flashing at every penetration. Our sheds are still standing and looking great 15 years after completion.
