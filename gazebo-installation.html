Our Approach

Timeless Design Principles

Great gazebos follow centuries-old proportioning rules: the ratio of post height to span, the pitch of the roof, the width of the railings. We apply these principles precisely to create structures that simply feel right from every angle.

Roofing Craftsmanship

The gazebo roof is its most prominent architectural feature. Whether asphalt shingles, cedar shake, or metal standing-seam, we install every roofing system with the same care and precision as a full house re-roof.

Deep Foundation Engineering

Ontario's frost depth demands that gazebo foundations go below the frost line to prevent heaving. We use poured concrete piers set at minimum 4 feet depth to anchor your structure permanently through every freeze-thaw cycle.

Screened Enclosures

Bug-free outdoor living is one of the greatest summer luxuries in the GTA. Our custom-framed screen systems use fibreglass mesh in quality frames that seal tightly and maintain their form and function for years without sagging or warping.

Electrical Integration

We rough in electrical during the framing stage for ceiling fans, pendant lighting, outdoor-rated outlets, and patio heaters. This approach avoids retrofitting and delivers a clean, invisible installation from day one.

Hot Tub Gazebo Specialists

Hot tub gazebos demand extra structural consideration for point load, humidity management, and ventilation design. We build these specifically with reinforced framing, moisture-resistant finishes, and properly sized ventilation openings.
